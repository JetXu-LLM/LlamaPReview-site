Welcome to LlamaPReview Support

Thank you for using LlamaPReview. This page reflects the current public service: LlamaPReview Advanced remains available for public open-source repositories, while free reviews for private repositories pause on May 1, 2026 00:00:00 UTC.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I install LlamaPReview?

A: You can install LlamaPReview directly from the GitHub Marketplace. Simply visit our GitHub App page and click on the "Install" button.

Q: What is currently available?

A: LlamaPReview Advanced remains free for public open-source repositories.

Q: What happens to private repository free reviews?

A: Free reviews for private repositories pause from May 1, 2026 00:00:00 UTC.

Q: Is LlamaPReview still being developed as a broader product?

A: No. We will not continue LlamaPReview as a broader product, while keeping the public open-source Advanced service available.

Q: Where can I follow the work from the same author now?

A: If you'd like to see our current open-source focus, you're welcome to take a look at DocMason.
