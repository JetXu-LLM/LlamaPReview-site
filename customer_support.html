LlamaPReview Customer Support

Welcome to LlamaPReview Support

Thank you for embarking on this incredible journey with us. We've witnessed over 4,000 repositories and 527 subscriptions trust LlamaPReview, which has been incredibly heartwarming.

As the AI coding ecosystem shifts dramatically towards autonomous "Vibe Coding" paradigms, we've had to make the difficult decision to step back from our original trajectory, acknowledging that the code review SaaS model as it stands has outlived its time.

Please note: While LlamaPReview Advanced remains fully operational and absolutely free for public open-source repositories, our free reviews for private repositories will permanently pause on May 1, 2026 00:00:00 UTC.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I install LlamaPReview?

A: You can install LlamaPReview directly from the GitHub Marketplace. Simply visit our GitHub App page and click on the "Install" button.

Q: Are free reviews for private repositories still supported?

A: No. We will pause free reviews for private repositories starting May 1, 2026 00:00:00 UTC.

Q: What is happening to the public open-source service?

A: Nothing changes for public open-source repositories! We will continue to support the open-source community by keeping LlamaPReview Advanced running for free on public repos.

Q: Is LlamaPReview still being developed actively?

A: Sadly, no. The wave of AI advancement has moved us to pivot. If you've loved our review tool and want to see what we're bringing to the new era of generative engineering, we invite you to check out DocMason, our newest open-source endeavor.
